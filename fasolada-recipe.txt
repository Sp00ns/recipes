Ingredients:
 - 500g dry white beans (18 ounces)
 - 2-3 carrots, finely chopped
 - 1 large red onion, finely chopped
 - 3 stalks of celery, finely chopped
 - 130ml extra virgin olive oil (1/2 cup)
 - 2 tbsps tomato puree
 - a pinch of paprika
 - salt and freshly ground pepper

Directions:
 1. Place beans in saucepan covered with cold water. Bring to boil. Turn heat
	to medium and parboil for 30-35 mins, until slightly tender. Drain and set
	aside.
 2. Finely chop onion, celery and carrots. Add 3-4 tbsps of olive oil in a deep
	pan, add chopped vegetables and blend. Saute for 2 mins and add tomato puree.
	Continue sauteing for a minutes
 3. Add beans and cover with boiling water. Blend lightly. Cover with lid and
	simmer for 35 mins until beans are tender.
 4. Towards end of cooking time add remaining olive oil and season.
